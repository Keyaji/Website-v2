Tools Used

Poster

I wanted to make a design that took elements from the current theme of the 2024 Olympics and simplify them into a recognizable form

When I researched the design inspiration around the 2024 Olympics, I found that they were using elements from art deco. With this I incorporated some of the established colours and focused the design around a graphic theme.

After some explorations, I went with a design simplifying the human form of rhythmic gymnastics. Using the ribbon the add a level of dimension and visual interest to the design.
